Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

Missile man of india

Dr. Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India (2002–2007) Known as the "Missile Man of India" for his pivotal role in India's missile development programs, he was a beloved leader, educator, and visionary. His humility, dedication to science, and passion for inspiring youth made him a national icon.

Achievements

  • President of India from 2002 to 2007
  • Led India's missile and nuclear weapons programs
  • Worked with ISRO and DRDO on space and defense technologies
  • Recipient of Bharat Ratna, Padma Bhushan, and Padma Vibhushan
  • Authored inspirational books like Wings of Fire and Ignited Minds
  • Dedicated his life to education and youth empowerment
